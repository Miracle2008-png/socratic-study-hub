# Water-Alternating-Gas (WAG) Processes

Miscible $CO_2$ is perfect at the microscopic level ($E_D \approx 1.0$), because it dissolves into the oil and destroys capillary forces. 

However, $CO_2$ is a gas. It has a catastrophic flaw at the macroscopic level ($E_A$ and $E_V$). 
*   **Viscosity:** The viscosity of $CO_2$ is astronomically low (around 0.05 cP). The Mobility Ratio ($M$) is terrible. It violently fingers through the oil, completely bypassing massive sections of the reservoir.
*   **Density:** $CO_2$ is very light. It violently floats to the top of the reservoir (Gravity Override), sweeping only the ceiling and ignoring the deep oil.

If you just pump a continuous stream of pure $CO_2$, the project will fail. The $CO_2$ will finger through the rock, hit the producing well in two weeks, and you will just be endlessly cycling useless gas through an empty channel.

To force the highly mobile $CO_2$ to behave, engineers must use physical barricades. This is the **Water-Alternating-Gas (WAG)** process.

## 1. The Mechanics of WAG

The WAG process is a cyclical injection strategy that alternates between a highly mobile gas and a heavy, viscous liquid.

1.  **The Gas Slug:** The engineer injects pure $CO_2$ for 3 to 6 months. The massive cloud of $CO_2$ enters the rock, achieves multiple-contact miscibility, dissolves the trapped residual oil droplets, and begins to violently finger ahead.
2.  **The Water Slug:** The engineer throws a massive surface valve, shutting off the $CO_2$ and instantly pumping heavy, viscous Saltwater into the exact same well for the next 3 to 6 months.
3.  **The Physical Barricade:** The massive slug of heavy water enters the rock right behind the $CO_2$. Because water is 20 times more viscous than $CO_2$, it acts like a solid, slow-moving physical wall. 
4.  **Sweep Optimization:** The heavy water wall smashes into the back of the highly mobile $CO_2$ fingers. It physically blocks the gas from rushing forward. The $CO_2$ is violently pushed outward, horizontally and vertically, forcing it to sweep perfectly across the entire geographic area of the reservoir, contacting massive amounts of untouched oil.
5.  **The Cycle Continues:** The engineer repeats this 6-month cycle continuously for the next 20 years.

By combining the microscopic chemical perfection of $CO_2$ with the macroscopic physical sweep efficiency of Water, the engineer achieves the ultimate high Overall Recovery Factor ($E_R$).

## 2. WAG Ratios and Tapering

Designing a WAG flood is incredibly complex. The engineer must decide the exact **WAG Ratio** (the volume of water injected divided by the volume of gas injected).

*   **1:1 Ratio:** Inject 1,000 barrels of water, then 1,000 reservoir-barrels of gas. This is standard.
*   **High Ratio (2:1):** If the rock is highly fractured and the gas is fingering too violently, you must inject twice as much water to act as a massive brake to slow the gas down.
*   **Tapering:** $CO_2$ is expensive, and water is cheap. In the first 5 years of the project, you might use a 1:1 ratio to aggressively attack the oil. But in year 15, when most of the oil is gone, you "taper" the flood to save money, injecting a massive 4:1 ratio (mostly cheap water with just tiny bursts of expensive $CO_2$ to sweep the final droplets).

## 3. Foams (The Evolution of WAG)

Even with WAG, Gravity Override is still a problem. The heavy water sinks to the bottom, and the light gas floats to the top. They physically separate underground, ruining the barricade effect.

To force the gas and water to physically stay together, engineers use **Foam WAG (FAWAG)**.
They inject a specialized Surfactant (soap) into the water slug. 

Deep underground, when the high-pressure gas violently mixes with the soapy water inside the microscopic rock pores, it acts exactly like a sponge full of dish soap. It generates a massive, thick, rigid foam.
*   The gas bubbles are trapped inside the rigid foam bubbles. 
*   This artificially increases the "Apparent Viscosity" of the gas by a factor of 1,000. 
*   The incredibly thick foam acts like a massive, unstoppable wall of shaving cream, perfectly sweeping the entire reservoir from top to bottom, completely eliminating Viscous Fingering and Gravity Override simultaneously.
